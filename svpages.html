ABOUT US

Welcome to the official page of the Sunshine Volunteers!
At our program, we value the health and wellbeing of youth through volunteering.
We encourage anyone who is 13 or older to join us in serving the city of Moncton
by picking up volunteering roles at various organizations. We believe this helps
to boost the health of those helping and those being helped through the kindness
and love we raise our volunteers to show. We also build a trust like family with
all the people we work with. We stand for love, kindness, peace, and helping out
even where help was not asked for as long as its needed.

We are a non-government organization and we accept donations.
